1. name: char field, take automatic sequence like (RG000001)
2. student_id: many2one on res.partner model ( filter on partner that has the check box is_student)
and when creating students on the ly please make sure that checkbox is checked by default.
(required*)
3. phone: related by student phone
4. age: computed ield that calculates student age from student birth date and checks for the 4-5
years constraint.
5. date: date field. (required*)
6. currency_id: many2one ield on res.currency model and will take company currency by default.
(readonly)
7. amount: (registration fees) monetary ield. (required*)
8. state: selection ield with the following values (draft – conirmed – invoiced - canceled)
